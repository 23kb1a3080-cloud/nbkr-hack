About the
NBKR Hackathon

Get all the details about our upcoming 24-hour non-stop on-campus building sprint.

NBKR Hackathon is a 24-hour offline hackathon open to students from NBKRIST and all other colleges — any branch, any year, any skill level. Every team participates. No eliminations before the event.

Organized by the Department of Artificial Intelligence & Data Science, NBKRIST, this event challenges students to move from raw idea to working prototype in one focused sprint — guided by industry mentors, with all meals fully provided, and the energy of the whole campus behind you.

Form a team of 2–4. Build something real in 24 hours. Walk out with a prototype, a certificate, and a story worth telling.

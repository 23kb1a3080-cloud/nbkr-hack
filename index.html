About the Event

What is NBKR Hackathon?

NBKR Hackathon is a 24-hour offline hackathon open to students from NBKRIST and all other colleges. Every team must be physically present — there is no virtual, remote, or hybrid option.

Organized by the Department of CSE, N.B.K.R. Institute of Science & Technology, this event invites students to move from idea to prototype in one sleepless sprint — guided by mentors, fed by the organizers, and cheered on by the whole campus.

Register as a team of 2–4. No shortlisting rounds — every registered team gets to participate. Hack for 24 hours straight.

Rules & Judging

How It Works

Eligibility & Conduct

  • Open to students from NBKRIST and all other colleges
  • Teams of 2–4 members
  • No shortlisting — every registered team participates
  • All development must start after the official kick-off
  • Pre-built projects lead to immediate disqualification
  • AI coding tools permitted but must be disclosed at submission
  • Any technology stack allowed — web, mobile, hardware, or hybrid
  • Final deliverable must be a working prototype, not a slide deck
  • Respectful conduct towards all participants is mandatory

Judging Criteria

Innovation & Creativity25%
Technical Complexity25%
Completeness & Functionality20%
Design & User Experience20%
Social / Practical Impact10%

Judging happens in two rounds — mid-point review and final on-stage presentation, both in person at NBKRIST.
